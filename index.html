Dice Game

Dice Game is a simple game based on rolling a die to get a random result between the numbers 1 and 6. The user can click the "Roll Dice" button to perform the roll and display the result on the screen. A visual effect of the die changes based on the number rolled. If the player rolls a 1, they lose all their current points in the "CURRENT" button, and the turn automatically switches to the other player. The game continues until one of the players reaches a SCORE of 100, at which point that player wins.>
